FAQ's (Frequently Asked Questions)

Order/Shipping Questions

  • When will my order be delivered?

    Shipping times will vary by region. Please expect slight delays due to COVID-19.

    REGION TIME
    CANADA 2 - 6 Business Days
    USA 2 - 10 Business Days
    Everywhere else 7 - 15 Business Days
  • Can I ship to an international address?

    Yes.

  • How will you ship my order?

    Your order will be shipped via tracked parcel through Canada Post.

  • What is the exchange/return policy?

    Please refer to our return policy here.

  • Will I receive a tracking number?

    Yes, you will be provided with a tracking number once your order has been packed. Please expect a slight delay in your tracking number being uploaded to Canada Post’s website.

  • What do I do if I haven’t received my order?

    If you haven’t received your order within the estimated time provided, please contact us at info@GetLoudforSickKids.ca with your order details.

  • What do I do if my order is defective?

    If you receive a defective or incorrect order, please contact us at info@GetLoudforSickKids.ca or 416-445-3377 and we will arrange for a replacement.

  • How do I make a donation to SickKids GetLoud?

    Thank you for your interest in donating to SickKids GetLoud! You can make your tax receiptable donation here.

GetLoud Fundraising Rewards

  • How do I earn fundraising rewards?

    Earn your fundraising rewards by fundraising for GetLoud on your personal page or team page and reaching the below milestones. Rewards are based on average individual fundraising. All funds raised and received by SickKids Foundation by September 30th will count towards your qualification level. Need help? Reach out to us at 416-445-3377 or info@GetLoudforSickKids.ca.

  • How do I redeem my fundraising reward(s)?

    Congratulations and thank you for all your fundraising efforts! To redeem your reward(s) please follow the steps below:

    1. Keep an eye on your inbox the week of October 12th for an email with the fundraising reward items you have qualified for and a reward pin code (please note, if multiple participants have registered with one email, all items will be grouped together and sent in one email).
    2. Click on the link to the GetLoud Shop provided in the email and login using the provided credentials.
    3. Select the items you qualified for (listed in the email), select any sizes if applicable and add to your cart.
    4. Check out using the reward pin code found in your email and enter your shipping address.
    5. Wait for your exciting delivery to arrive!
  • How do fundraising rewards work for teams?

    We recognize that some teams prefer to pool fundraising together. Fundraising rewards are earned based on your personal fundraising, as well as your team fundraising. For example, to earn the $300 reward, your team would need to raise an average of $300 per person. A team of 4 people would need to raise a team total of $1,200 for all team members to earn the $300 fundraising reward item.

  • How long do I have to order my Fundraising Reward Items?

    You have until November 30, 2020, to redeem your fundraising reward items.

  • Can I purchase fundraising reward items?

    No, fundraising reward items can only be redeemed by hitting the associated fundraising levels.
